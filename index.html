CFRMS

Church Financial Records Management System

Lahing Pinagpala Nation Church

The Church Financial Records Management System (CFRMS) is an automated database management platform designed to replace manual, paper-based logbooks. This documentation platform presents the comprehensive results of the prototype's usability evaluation through Heuristic Analysis and Usability Testing.
