CFRMS

Church Financial Records Management System

Lahing Pinagpala Nation Church

The Church Financial Records Management System (CFRMS) is an moobile-based application developed exclusively for use by the Lahing Pinagpala Nation Church. CFRMS provides a shift from manual and inefficient record keeping using paper logbooks to an automated database management system.
