Team Reflections

Transforming the CFRMS Prototype into a documented platform allowed us to bridge the gap between technical design and user-centered reality. The most significant realization during the Heuristic Evaluation was how easily design team members can overlook simple usability flaws that actual users find immediately.

Conducting testing with BSMath students taught us the value of "Natural Match"; while we used technical terms, our users looked for accounting language they recognized. This project has strengthened our front-end development skills and our understanding of Jakob Nielsen’s 10 Heuristics as a vital tool for preventing real-world system failure.
